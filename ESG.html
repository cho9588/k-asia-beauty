What is ESG Management?


'ESG' refers to the non-financial factors, Environment, Social, Governance.

'ESG Management' is a pursuit of sustainable development through eco-friendly, CSR, and transparent management in the long term perspective.



ESG Management Strategies of K-Asia Beauty

Major Challenges of K-Asia Beauty


① Increase in purchase ratio of green products
② Reducing of carbon, greenhouse gas, and other toxic substances
③ Reduction energy consumption and improving energy efficiency
④ Efforts to reduce event wastes
⑤ Earth Check Certification GOLD for 4 consecutive years

① Improvement of staff and customer satisfaction
② Improvement of industrial accident management and employment welfare
③ Expansion and reinforcement of social contribution

① Increase in social fairness and fairness in executive selection
② Reinforcement of prevention against unjust actions and corruptions
③ Improvement of ethical management policies
④ Managing public institution integrity value
